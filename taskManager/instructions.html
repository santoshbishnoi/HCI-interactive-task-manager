    Instructions


  • To add new task , click on “ ADD TASK “ on the top right corner

  • To navigate to Home Screen , Click on “ INTERACTIVE TASK MANAGER “ on the top left of the screen

  • To clear all the current tasks, Click on “CLEAR ALL” button in the bottom left of the screen

  • It is mandatory to fill all the details asked while adding a new task and editing an existing task.
